Weekly Grocery List

Buy this once a week. Everything is available at a standard supermarket.

Proteins

  • Chicken breast (3 lbs)
  • Lean ground beef (1 lb)
  • Ground turkey (1 lb)
  • Salmon fillets (4)
  • Eggs (18-pack)
  • Greek yogurt, 0% (4 large tubs)
  • Cottage cheese (1 lb tub)
  • Tinned tuna (6 cans)

Carbs

  • Basmati or jasmine rice (2 lbs)
  • Rolled oats (2 lbs)
  • Sweet potatoes (2 lbs)
  • Whole wheat bread (1 loaf)
  • Pasta (1 lb)
  • Corn tortillas (pack)
  • Rice cakes (pack)

Veg & Fruit

  • Broccoli (2 heads)
  • Baby spinach (large bag)
  • Bell peppers (6)
  • Cherry tomatoes (punnet)
  • Onions (bag)
  • Apples (bag)
  • Blueberries or mixed berries
  • Frozen mixed veg (bag)

Fats & Flavour

  • Olive oil
  • Avocados (4–5)
  • Almonds or mixed nuts
  • Peanut butter (natural)
  • Garlic (bulb)
  • Soy sauce (low sodium)
  • Hot sauce

Pantry Staples

  • Chicken/beef stock cubes
  • Tinned chopped tomatoes
  • Black beans (tinned)
  • Smoked paprika
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Honey (small jar)

Drinks

  • Sparkling water (bulk)
  • Black coffee or green tea
  • Protein powder (optional)
  • Skimmed or semi-skimmed milk

Weekly Workout Template

Repeat this schedule both weeks. Lift days: add weight when 3 sets of 12 feels easy.

```
MON
Push Day
40 min · Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
Lift
▼
01
Barbell Bench Press
3 sets × 8–10 reps
Lower to chest, pause briefly, drive up
02
Dumbbell Shoulder Press
3 sets × 10–12 reps
Don't lock elbows at top
03
Incline Dumbbell Press
3 sets × 10–12 reps
30–45° incline
04
Cable Tricep Pushdown
3 sets × 12–15 reps
Keep elbows pinned to sides
05
Plank
3 × 30–45 sec
Squeeze glutes and core together
TUE
Pull Day
40 min · Back, Biceps
Lift
▼
01
Lat Pulldown
3 sets × 10–12 reps
Pull to upper chest, squeeze at bottom
02
Seated Cable Row
3 sets × 10–12 reps
Drive elbows back, hold 1 sec
03
Single-Arm Dumbbell Row
3 sets × 10 reps each
Brace on bench, row to hip
04
Face Pulls
3 sets × 15 reps
External rotation at end of pull
05
Dumbbell Curl
3 sets × 12 reps
Slow on the way down
WED
Zone 2 Cardio
30 min · Low Intensity
Cardio
▼
How to do it:Treadmill incline walk (incline 8–12, speed 3–4 mph), stationary bike, or elliptical. You should be able to hold a conversation — not gasping, not strolling. Stay in that zone the full 30 minutes. This burns fat and aids recovery without beating up your joints.

▼

3 sets × 8–10 reps
Core tight, don't arch lower back

SAT
Zone 2 Cardio (Optional)
30 min · Easy
Cardio
▼
How to do it:Same as Wednesday — 30 minutes at a pace where you can talk. Genuinely optional. If you're wiped out, take the rest day. If you feel good, this extra session accelerates fat loss meaningfully over 12 weeks.
SUN
Rest & Recovery
No training
Rest
▼
Full rest. Get 7–8 hours sleep — this is when your muscles grow and fat loss works best. Use today to do your weekly meal prep so Monday starts with zero friction.
```
Progressive Overload — The One Rule Every week, try to add a little weight or one extra rep to each lift. This is how you keep muscle while cutting. Don't ego-lift — go heavy enough that the last 2 reps are genuinely hard.
