Weekly Workout Template

Repeat this schedule both weeks. Lift days: add weight when 3 sets of 12 feels easy.

Progressive Overload — The One Rule Every week, try to add a little weight or one extra rep to each lift. This is how you keep muscle while cutting. Don't go heavy enough to ego-lift — go heavy enough that the last 2 reps are genuinely hard.
